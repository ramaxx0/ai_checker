Security & Privacy

Local-Only Analysis

All password checks are performed directly in your browser using JavaScript. Your password never leaves your device and is not sent to any server.

No Storage

The application does not log, store, or cache your password. Any metrics shown on screen are derived in memory and cleared when you refresh or close the page.

What Is Stored

  • Optional: your dark mode preference, stored in your browser’s local storage.
  • No analytics, tracking scripts, or external calls are required for the checker to function.

Educational Use

This tool is designed for educational and awareness purposes. It cannot guarantee the security of any specific password in all contexts. Always follow your organization’s password policy.
